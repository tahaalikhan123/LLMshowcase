Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Language Model?

A Language Model is an AI system trained on vast amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like text.

How do LLMs work?

LLMs use deep learning techniques, typically based on transformer architectures, to process and generate text based on patterns learned from training data.

Are LLMs safe to use?

While LLMs are generally safe, they should be used responsibly. They can sometimes produce biased or incorrect information, so human oversight is important.
